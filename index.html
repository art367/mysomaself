Wellness Session Prep

Before your sauna, cold plunge, or any heat therapy — prime your body for deeper detox.

💧 Hydration First

Drink at least 500ml of water before you enter heat. Add a pinch of sea salt or electrolytes. Your lymphatic system needs fluid to flush effectively.

Lymphatic Activation Sequence

Tap each area 10–15 times with gentle fingertip pressure. Tick each one off as you go.

  • Collarbone — tap both sides of the notch
  • Armpits — light fingertip tapping
  • Groin — crease between hip and thigh, both sides
  • Behind the knees — gentle tapping, both legs

✦ Biohack Boost

Dry-brush your skin before the sauna in upward strokes toward your heart. This primes your lymphatic vessels and enhances the detox response by up to 30%.

During & After

Exit the heat slowly — sit on the edge for 60 seconds before standing. Rehydrate with water + coconut water. Rest for 10 minutes before cold exposure if you're alternating.

My body is a self-cleaning system. I am supporting it, not forcing it.
