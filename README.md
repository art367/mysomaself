## The Story

This didn't start as a game. It started as a vision — a blueprint for something rare: an app that doesn't ask you to improve, track, or optimise. Just *return*.

Sally had sketched out an entire philosophy. Four states of self: **Arriving**, **Sensing**, **Releasing**, **Returning**. A somatic check-in that reads your felt sense instead of your mood. Breathing guided not by a timer, but by an orb that expands like a living thing. Moments of return instead of streaks. A glowing core of self that grows quieter, not louder.

## What It Is

A somatic journey in four phases you move through each session:

| Phase | Intent |
|-------|--------|
| 🌅 **Arriving** | Touch body points to check in — crown, heart, hands, feet |
| 🌿 **Sensing** | Felt-sense sliders: heavy↔light, tight↔open, restless↔calm |
| 🌙 **Releasing** | A breathing orb guides 3 slow breaths — inhale, hold, exhale |
| 🌌 **Returning** | Words appear slowly. You say: *I am here.* |

Your **Moments of Return** accumulate as a soft constellation. No streaks. No scores. Just a record of the times you came back to yourself.

---

## What's Inside

- Canvas 2D atmosphere with floating warm/violet motes and radial glow that shifts per phase
- SVG body silhouette with 8 tappable soma points, ripple effects on activation
- Felt-sense guidance engine matching slider states to 10 somatic messages
- Breathing orb with 4s inhale / 1s hold / 5s exhale cadence, 3 cycles
- Fade-in returning ritual with staggered line reveals
- `localStorage` persistence — your constellation grows across sessions
- Mobile + desktop: mouse and touch, responsive layout, no frameworks
